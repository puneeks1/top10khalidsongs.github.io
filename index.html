About Khalid

Khalid Donnel Robinson, known mononymously as Khalid, is an American singer and songwriter. Born on February 11, 1998, in Fort Stewart, Georgia, Khalid experienced a rapid rise to fame with his debut single "Location" in 2016, which was later certified quadruple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

His music is characterized by its soothing melodies and introspective lyrics, often exploring themes of youth, love, and heartbreak. Khalid's debut studio album, "American Teen," released in 2017, received widespread acclaim and earned him several Grammy Award nominations. Since then, Khalid has continued to captivate audiences with his soulful voice and relatable storytelling, solidifying his place as one of the leading voices in contemporary R&B and pop music.
